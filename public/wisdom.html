Wisdom, Knowledge & Decision Making

Talo saaxiib baa lagu doortaa

Meaning: A friend is chosen for their wisdom

Explanation: This emphasizes the value of wisdom in friendship and decision-making

Xikmad la'aan xoolo laguma helo

Meaning: Without wisdom, one cannot acquire wealth

Explanation: This shows the importance of intelligence in achieving success and wealth

Nin walba wuxuu beertay buu guranayaa

Meaning: Every man reaps what he sows

Explanation: This highlights the idea that one's actions will have consequences

Haddii aad rabto in aad fog gaarto, socodkaaga ha dedejin

Meaning: If you want to go far, do not rush your steps

Explanation: This advises patience and perseverance in achieving long-term goals

Haddii aad rabto in laguu waramo, dad ha warin.

Meaning: If you want people to keep your secrets, don't reveal others' secrets

Explanation: This stresses the value of confidentiality and trustworthiness

Meel hoos loo dhigo, hoos baa loo hela

Meaning: Lower something, and you will find shelter

Explanation: This encourages humility and stresses its importance in finding respect and protection
