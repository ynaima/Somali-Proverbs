Beauty & Character

Quruxi waa ishaada, qiimana waa ayaankaaga

Meaning: Beauty is in the eye of the beholder

Explanation: External beauty may be subjective, but inner worth and character define a person's true value

Qofkii aragiisu ku deeqaa oontiisuna ku deeqdaa

Meaning: The one whose presence pleases you also satisfies your thirst

Explanation: Some people bring both emotional and practical fulfillment

Nin qurux badan naagtaada uga yaab nin fool xuna naftaada uga yaab

Meaning: A beautiful man is a threat to your wife, a hideous one to your soul

Explanation: Physical beauty can be deceptive. A handsome man may be dangerous for your relationship, while the unattractive one might be a threat to your self-esteem

Qalbigu laba ruux ma wada qaado

Meaning: You can't love two people at the same time

Explanation: True love and loyalty cannot be divided between two individuals

Naagaha iyo carruurtaba sasabaa lagu wadaa

Meaning: Women and children are best led with kindness

Explanation: This stresses that kindness and patience are essential in guiding and nurturing others, particularly women and children

Naagtii caana xun dhigataa iyana ku murud la'

Meaning: A woman who spills milk must live with the smell

Explanation: This proverb stresses personal responsibility. If you create problems, you cannot complain about the consequences
