Humility & Community

Safar wa sida wada yeel

Meaning: A journey is best taken together

Explanation: Collaboration and unity makes any task or journey more successful

Nin waalan tolkii ba umiyir qaba

Meaning: A mad man's family keeps him sane

Explanation: This speaks to the importance of Community and support systems. In times of trouble or instability, the community helps to keep individuals grounded

Nin walba meshii buktaa belbesha

Meaning: Every man scratches where it itches

Explanation: People's action are often self-serving, driven by personal needs or comfort

Naago waa belo loo baahan yahay

Meaning: Women are a necessary challenge

Explanation: This suggests that women can be a challenge, but they are integral and necessary in society, highlighting their importance in community life

Somalidu waxay casarkii kuguaragtay cishihii kugu caydaa

Meaning: People judge you by what they see

Explanation: How you behave at a particular time can determine how others see you. Your actions are judged in context

Seedi waa beer

Meaning: A brother-in-law is like a liver

Explanation: This proverb stresses on the important of relationships by marriage. That they should be valued and respected
