Responsibility & Consequences

Nin xaal qaaday eed qaad

Meaning: He who takes on responsibility must bear the blame

Explanation: Leadership and responsibility come with accountability

Nimaan qaybin jiray haduu qaybiyo nimaan qadi jirin baa qada

Meaning: He who has never shared before will distribute unfairly

Explanation: Those who don't practice generosity may only share out of selfish motives

Nimaan dhididin ma dhergo

Meaning: No pain no gain

Explanation: Hard work is essential to achieving success. Without effort, there is no reward

Far Keliya fool ma dhaqdo

Meaning: One finger cannot wash a face

Explanation: Teamwork is necessary, no one can succeed alone

Nimaan shaqaysan shaxaad baa dila

Meaning: He who does not work is doomed to begging

Explanation: Reliance on others without personal effort leads to deterioration. It's a call to contribute and work for one's sustenance

Lax walba meeshay is dhigtaa lagu gowracaa

Meaning: As you make your bed, so you must lie in it

Explanation: This proverb emphasizes on personal responsibility and consequences. It means one faces the consequences of their own choices and actions
