Trust, Honesty & Integrity

Aamin dumay dib uma soo laabto

Meaning: Trust, once broken, never returns

Explanation: Once trust is lost, it is hard to restore.

Nin ku necebi xantaa og, nin ku jeclina xogtaa og

Meaning: Your enemy knows your faults, your friend knows your secrets

Explanation: True friends know you deeply, while enemies only know superficial things about you

Ballan-darro waa diin-darro

Meaning: Breaking a promise is a lack of faith

Explanation: Failing to keep your word reflects poorly on your morals and integrity

Hadal iyo hilibba waa la qaybiyaa

Meaning: Words, like meat, should be shared fairly

Explanation: Honesty and fairness in speech are just as important as fairness in sharing resources

Nin aan talin, waa la toogtaa

Meaning: A man who does not make decisions will be ruled over

Explanation: It stresses the importance of responsibility and the consequences of indecision

Runtu way qurxoontahay, beenuse lug ma leh

Meaning: Truth is beautiful, but a lie has no legs to stand on

Explanation: The truth is always respected, while lies eventually collapse
