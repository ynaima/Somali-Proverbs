Action Vs Words

Hadal yar iyo hawl badan baa la amaanaa

Meaning: Few words and much work are praiseworthy

Explanation: Actions are valued more than words; hard work speaks louder

Hadal badan haan ma buuxsho

Meaning: Too much talk does not fill the granary

Explanation: Talking alone does not lead to results—action is what matters

Libaax labadiisa lugood ayaa la kaluumeystaa

Meaning:A lion hunts with its own two legs

Explanation: You must take action yourself instead of relying on others

Fadhi iyo feejignaan waa laba is diidan

Meaning: Sitting and being alert are two opposing things

Explanation: One cannot claim to be ready for action while remaining idle

Gacmo furan iyo af xiran baa la isku ammaanay

Meaning: Open hands and a closed mouth are praised

Explanation: Generosity and silent hard work are more valuable than boasting

Orod iyo hadal isma qabtaan

Meaning: Running and talking do not go together

Explanation: You cannot focus on action while wasting time talking
